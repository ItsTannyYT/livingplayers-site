Living Players

A Minecraft Fabric mod that adds autonomous AI player-like entities with memory, needs, inventory, learning, chat responses, and survival behavior.

Explore Features Technical Details Get Mod on Patreon
🧠

AI Player Features

  • AI Player Entity: Adds player-like AI characters, not villagers.
  • Persistent Identity: AI players can keep identity, memory, inventory, and knowledge.
  • Survival Behavior: AI can gather wood, craft basics, sleep, flee danger, and hold items visually.
  • Natural Chat: Designed for chat commands, delayed replies, and player interaction.
⚙️

Technical Specifications

  • Minecraft: 1.21.1
  • Mod Loader: Fabric Loader 0.19.3
  • Fabric API: 0.116.12+1.21.1
  • Language: Java 21
  • Mod ID: livingplayers
  • Package: com.livingplayers

Development Roadmap

Current systems include AI brain ticks, basic survival priorities, memory, known beds, known chests, wood gathering, crafting, sleep behavior, fleeing, and item handling.

Next planned systems include item classification, stronger food gathering, chest ownership rules, better storage behavior, and expanded long-term AI decision making.
